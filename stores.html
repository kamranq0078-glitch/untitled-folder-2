Getting Here

From Sheikh ul-Alam Airport, take the NH44 toward Artisan Boutique city centre. Head toward Zunnamar — we are located on Dr Ali Jan Road, Near Shah-i-Hamdan Colony, a well-known landmark in the area. Most taxi drivers know it. A pre-arranged pickup from your hotel is available on request via WhatsApp.
